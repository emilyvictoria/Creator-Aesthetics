Hannah Hart

Hannah Hart is YouTube royalty, and she understands the value of a perfect Internet aesthetic. What does this mean for her? It's a combination of branding, ease-of-access, and feel-good imagery for her. Come hear her speak on the evolution of her YouTube channel for insight on the progression of such an influential platform.


Ingrid Burrington

Ingrid Burrington is an expert on the Internet's infrastructure and the design, politics, and artistry of the Web. She'll be contributing to Creator Aesthetic's understanding of the Internet as a structurally artistic platform for creators everywhere.


Susan Kare

Susan Kare was one of the formatic Apple designers during the 1980s, and today she has left her influence on the Web from interface design to typefaces. Her work allows creators the freedom to explore the artistry of the Internet becauase of her own creator aesthetics.


Laura Poitras

Laura Poitras, 2015 Academy Award winner, has directed films and documentaries that have heavily influenced the American ideal of documentary work. She's thrilled to explore the aesthetics of truth and the visuals that allow one to tell a story.


Zoë Quinn

Zoë Quinn is a video game developer, programmer, writer, and artist that contributes to the most interactive of content design. She thrives on the immersive, artistic elements of game design, and at Creator Aesthetics she'll unveil some of her most valuable lessons from designing immersive experiences.
